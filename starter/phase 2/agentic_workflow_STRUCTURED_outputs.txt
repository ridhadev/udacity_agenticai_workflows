**USER STORIES**:

As a Customer Support Representative, I want the Email Router system to automatically categorize incoming emails based on their content, so that I can focus on addressing complex customer inquiries rather than spending time on routine tasks.

As an IT Administrator, I need the Email Router system to seamlessly integrate with our existing email infrastructure via SMTP, IMAP, and RESTful APIs, in order to ensure smooth operation and data flow within our organization.

As a Subject Matter Expert (SME), I expect the Email Router system to intelligently route complex inquiries to me based on content analysis and defined business rules, so that I can efficiently address specialized customer needs without being overwhelmed by irrelevant messages.

As a Team Leader, I require the Email Router system to provide a management dashboard for monitoring system performance, workflow bottlenecks, and response metrics, enabling me to track the efficiency and effectiveness of our email communication processes.

As a Compliance Officer, I seek assurance that the Email Router system complies with data protection regulations such as GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant standards, ensuring that sensitive information is handled securely and in accordance with legal requirements.

**PRODUCT FEATURES**:

**Email Organization Features:**

1. **Feature Name: Email Filtering**
   - **Description:** Automatically sorts incoming emails into different folders based on pre-defined rules.
   - **Key Functionality:** Rule-based filtering, customizable rules, folder organization.
   - **User Benefit:** Saves time by organizing emails efficiently, reduces clutter in the inbox. For example, important work emails can be directed to a specific folder for quick access.

2. **Feature Name: Email Scheduling**
   - **Description:** Allows users to schedule emails to be sent at a later time or date.
   - **Key Functionality:** Scheduling interface, date and time selection, draft management.
   - **User Benefit:** Helps users manage their communication more effectively, ensures timely delivery of emails. For instance, users can draft emails during non-working hours and schedule them to be sent during business hours.

3. **Feature Name: Email Tracking**
   - **Description:** Provides read receipts and tracking information for sent emails.
   - **Key Functionality:** Read receipt notifications, tracking history, analytics.
   - **User Benefit:** Allows users to know when their emails are read, helps in tracking important communications. For example, users can follow up on emails that have not been opened to ensure timely responses.

**Email Efficiency Features:**

4. **Feature Name: Email Templates**
   - **Description:** Pre-designed email templates for quick and easy drafting of common emails.
   - **Key Functionality:** Template library, customization options, easy insertion.
   - **User Benefit:** Saves time on repetitive emails, ensures consistency in communication. For instance, users can use templates for responses to frequently asked questions, saving time on composing similar emails repeatedly.

5. **Feature Name: Email Encryption**
   - **Description:** Secures email content through encryption to protect sensitive information.
   - **Key Functionality:** Encryption algorithms, secure key management, decryption process.
   - **User Benefit:** Ensures privacy and confidentiality of email communication, protects sensitive data from unauthorized access. For example, users can securely exchange confidential information without the risk of interception.
